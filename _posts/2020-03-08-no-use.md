You dwell in unapproachable brilliance <br>
I cannot see You <br>

<br>

You work in all things <br>
I cannot understand You <br>

<br>

Your goodness never ends <br>
I am overcome with dread <br>

<br>

When I behold You <br>
I am worthless <br>
You are that You are <br>
Without me <br>
