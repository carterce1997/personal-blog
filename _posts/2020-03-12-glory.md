title: Glory
author: Chris Carter
---

As I walk <br>
I watch the stars worship You <br>
And I hear the stones sing Your praises. <br>

<br>

But my eyes are glass <br>
Destitution is my air <br>
And sorrow aches in my bones. <br>

<br>

I sing empty words. <br>
And I long to see You <br>
As You are. <br>
